Shiro Wat

Shiro wat is made from chickpea and broad bean flour, mixed with garlic and onions, and made into a thick, almost paste like substance. Kind of like refried beans but smoother.

misir wot with Tomato Salad

The lentils are cooked with a few spoons of berbere spice powder to give them a nice redness in color, and cooked until tender, yet they still have some texture to them.The tomatoes are diced, mixed with some onions and chilies, and seasoned with salt, lemon juice, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Injera Fir Fir
